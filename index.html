PHP-NUKE-to-phpBB3-convertor by ameeck

Converter plug-in for the phpBB3 system to transfer data from existing NUKE phpBB2 ports.

The convertor enables you to transfer all your: - posts - users - forums to a new phpBB3 installation. The board will not be connected to PHP-NUKE anymore, though bridging is possible with a third-party plug-in

Dependencies

phpBB3

Install

Upload the install/ directory to the phpBB3 root. Then go to http://www.yourboard.com/install/ and select Convert at the top. Select the Universal PHP-NUKE converter and continue as the wizard instructs you.
